About

DEXTA DAPS

Jamaican reggae and dancehall singer Dexta Daps started to make waves in the music scene with his singles Morning Love, Shabba Madda Pot and Jealous Over. After many successful singles Dexta Daps released his first album INTRO (2017). Released No Underwear, Owner and Breaking News.

Following his success with his first released album Dexta Daps formed his own record label Dexta Daps Music Group and independently released his second album VENT(2020) that has surpassed 40 million streams and peaked at #10 on the Billboards reggae album charts. WiFi produced by Grammy award winning producer Drummaboy and working with Beenie Man, Tory Lanez, Davido, Spice, M.I.A. A proud native of the Seaview Gardens neighborhood in West Kingston.
